The Deep Above

A cross-media student project

The Project

The Deep Above is a cross-media project made by students of the Filmakademie Baden-Württemberg GmbH. Cross-media means that out of the IP two projects emerged: a game and a film.

My Role

In this very big cross-media student project, I was the Lead Technical Director. I was leading a team of 5 programmers, communicating with directors and producers, and implementing mechanics and the animation system into the game. Additionally, I supervised the MoCap shoot for Locomotion data and researched ways to implement (Learned) Motion Matching algorithms into the Engine, trained on our own MoCap data.
